Career Details

Title: Website Development

Total Cert POints Required: 3000

Duration: Six Months

Cost: FREE

Website Developers design, create, and maintain websites, focusing on front-end and back-end development. They handle various programming languages, frameworks, and design principles to create functional and visually appealing websites. Job opportunities include positions in software companies, digital agencies, startups, and freelancing. Website Developers can work as Front-end Developers, Back-end Developers, Full- stack Developers, or specialize in areas like UI/ UX design. The salary range can vary depending on experience, location, and expertise, ranging from 1.5 million to 12 million Naira per year
